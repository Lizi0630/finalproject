Our Psychiatric Treatments

We offer a comprehensive range of innovative and evidence-based psychiatric treatments, tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. Explore our therapies below, each supported by research and statistics, to understand how we can help you or your loved one.

TMS Therapy

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is a non-invasive treatment for depression, anxiety, OCD, and other mental health conditions. It uses magnetic fields to stimulate specific areas of the brain and has shown efficacy in patients who haven't responded to medications.

Key Statistic: A Stanford study found that nearly 80% of patients with severe depression achieved rapid remission through TMS, significantly higher than traditional treatments.

Ketamine Therapy

Ketamine therapy is an innovative treatment for severe depression, PTSD, and chronic anxiety. Administered in controlled doses under medical supervision, it can provide rapid relief from symptoms when other treatments have failed.

Key Statistic: In a clinical study, 52% of participants achieved remission from severe depression after three ketamine infusions over 11 days.

Medication Management

Our psychiatrists conduct thorough assessments and ongoing evaluations to determine the most effective medications and dosages for each individual. We monitor side effects, effectiveness, and work collaboratively with patients to adjust treatment plans as needed.

Key Statistic: According to the CDC, 16.5% of U.S. adults used prescription medication for mental health in 2020.
